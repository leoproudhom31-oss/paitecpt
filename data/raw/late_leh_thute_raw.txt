Poetry ) is to meet the urgent need for the requirement of the syllabus
for the Modern Indian Language Paper I! in Paite language.

Syllabus of the Manipur University. The syllabus and unit- wise division

NAU-OIH LA

Nau-oih La i cih naungek leh naupang neute khim
ihmutna la ahi hi. Nau puaklai, lupna tung a luppih lai Jeh
nauluai a vei lai a khim ihmutna dia naudon in asak ahi hi.
Nau khimnaa sak ahih louh ciang in mi khat sang a
tamzaw in a uun leh a eu in kisa hi. Tumging bangmah
thuah lou a sak ahi hi.
1 Bawiaw, hong khang mengmeng aw,

Pam a luidawng hong bang aw;

Nu’ tha khek aw pa’ tha dawn aw, tuai aw e.

Nu’ tha lah khekzou la’ng e, pa’ tha lah dawnzou la’ng e.
2 Innsungpi a sikha aw,

HAAM LA

Haam i cih kho hausapa’ inn hiam mi neizou kua
hiam khat inn, adiak in, innpua a sumtawng kiang a tangval
hon giahkhawmna ahi hi. Haam a giakkhawm sawm
tangvalte’n nitaak lengla pai ciang leh ziingsang khovak
Kuan in Haam La sa sek uh hi. Lup nung leh ziingthoh ma
a sak ahih ziak in tumging leh kaihging kithuah ngei lou hi.
Haam a giak peuhmah ina sakkhawm uh ahi. Mi nih hiam
ahihkeileh huai sang a tamzaw hiam ina eu khat Jeh a uun

a sak ahi.

I

(a) Taikhua hong val giilgial e zawl lengthou aw,

Na bu-algia huihkhita’n seem ngelngel e;
Na bu-algiai na simna hiam ci'ng e,
Taang hi ve’ng e laithah ka sial na hie.

(b) Bek-haam sian sung a va sung um bang kholhna,
Nuihciam simthu lentualkhia hi zou hang e;
Nuthciam simthu tualkhiak leh leenna tualkhiak ,
Guabang hin a lung a ngilh lou diing hi’ng e.

(c) Tai khoval kuan bu-al pau huihkhi dam laang,

-Ka sialcing toh maimit siing moh ngawn ing e;
Sialcing vul niingniang ing seinou vual aw,
Single! heisa saannem in phawng hie.
